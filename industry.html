Industry Overview

The fashion retail industry focuses on selling clothing and accessories to consumers. It is a fast-paced industry that responds to trends, customer demand, and seasonal changes.

Career of Interest: Retail Buyer

A retail buyer selects and purchases merchandise that will be sold in stores. Buyers analyze trends, sales data, and customer preferences to make purchasing decisions.

Ethical Issue in Fashion Retail

One major ethical issue in fashion retail is labor practices. Retail buyers must consider whether products are sourced ethically and produced under fair working conditions.

How My Personal Values Guide Ethical Decisions

My values of loyalty, respect, kindness, and integrity influence how I respond to ethical challenges. Integrity and respect guide me to support ethical brands, while kindness encourages consideration for workers. Loyalty motivates long-term partnerships with responsible suppliers.

Cybersecurity Issues in Fashion Retail

Retail buyers often work with sensitive data such as supplier contracts, pricing information, and customer data. Cybersecurity threats like data breaches or phishing attacks can put this information at risk.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Best practices include using secure passwords, protecting vendor data, limiting access to sensitive information, and following company cybersecurity policies to prevent data breaches.
